About Fundamental analysis

This course helps you understand the most important parameters from the financial statements such as balance sheet, income statement and cash flow statement. A Fundamental Analyst tries interpreting financial ratios to analyze operating activities, investing activities, liquidity, solvency and profitability. Get a broad understanding of interest rates, inflation and central banks and how they affect the market and the companies in focus. We start by going through the income statement, then the balance sheet, and lastly the cash flow statement. The skills learned in this course will help you determine whether a company has good financial health and if it is a good investment.
